Common Business Problems

Does Any of This Sound Familiar?

If you recognise even one of these challenges in your business, it is time for us to have a conversation. These everyday problems are quietly costing you far more than you realise.

Shift Reports on Paper, Data Missing by Morning

The supervisor recorded production numbers — but how accurate are they? Nobody truly knows what happened on the floor until it is far too late to act.

⚠ 2–3 hours lost every day just finding and reconciling data

A Machine Stopped — You Found Out 2 Hours Later

No one was watching. No alert was sent. Two hours of lost output and missed targets — all because there was no system in place to notify the right people in real time.

⚠ Average ₹15–50L/year lost to unplanned downtime

Energy Bills Keep Climbing With No Explanation

Your electricity bill grows every month, but which machines or processes are responsible? Is power being consumed during idle time? Without data, there are no answers.

⚠ 15–25% energy is typically wasted in unmonitored operations

Too Much Rework, Too Many Returns, Unhappy Clients

Defects are slipping through despite manual checks. Quality issues are inconsistent and hard to trace back to a root cause. Customer confidence is eroding.

⚠ 3–8% rejection rates translate to crores in annual losses

No Visibility Into Stock or Material Movement

Is raw material available? Has it reached the production line on time? Urgent orders are delayed because no one has a live view of inventory and material flow.

⚠ 10–20% excess inventory held due to poor visibility

Audit Prep Takes Days of Frantic Manual Work

Every compliance audit triggers a scramble — pulling records from scattered spreadsheets, filling in gaps, and hoping nothing is missed. There has to be a better way.

⚠ Non-compliance penalties and missed contracts at stake
