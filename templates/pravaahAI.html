How It Works

From Zero to Meeting
in Six AI Steps

PravaahAI runs an autonomous, intelligent pipeline that transforms cold markets into warm conversations — without your team lifting a finger.

Step 01

AI Finds Leads

Our AI engine continuously crawls trade databases, LinkedIn, import-export registries, and industry directories — surfacing thousands of verified decision-maker profiles matching your ideal customer profile.

Step 02

AI Enriches Data

Each contact is automatically enriched with verified emails, WhatsApp numbers, company financials, buying signals, LinkedIn profiles, and industry-specific context — giving your outreach maximum intelligence.

Step 03

AI Qualifies Prospects

The qualification engine scores every lead on intent, fit, budget signals and timing. Only high-probability prospects enter your active pipeline — eliminating wasted effort on poor-fit targets.

Step 04

AI Starts Outreach

Simultaneous, hyper-personalized outreach across Email, WhatsApp and Voice — crafted by AI to match each prospect's language, industry and role. Multi-touch sequences that feel human, never robotic.

Step 05

AI Books Meetings

When a prospect shows interest, AI handles the scheduling conversation, detects timezone, syncs your calendar and sends confirmation — the meeting appears in your diary without any manual work.

Step 06

Team Closes Deals

Your sales team enters the conversation with a warm, briefed, qualified prospect — armed with full context, conversation history and AI-suggested talking points. Close rates soar. Cycles shorten.
